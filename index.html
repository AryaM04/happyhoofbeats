One to One Sessions

Private sessions are a great way for individuals with anxiety and additional support needs to spend quality time with our ponies. Their calming nature helps to reduce stress and build confidence. Sessions are tailored to meet the needs of each individual. This can include grooming, walking in the countryside, making up feeds, filling hay nets etc.

These sessions are ¬£40 for 45 minutes.
